## How to use this roadmap
Every day has 3 parts:
- **Concept** — what to understand
- **Practice** — what to build/code
- **Deliverable** — what must exist by the end of the day

If a day is missed:
- mark it as **Pending**
- recover it on the next buffer/revision day
- do not restart the roadmap from zero
